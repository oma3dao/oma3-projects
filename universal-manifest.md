# Universal Manifest — The Missing Permission Layer

The web has TCP/IP for packets, HTTPS for encrypted traffic, DNS for names. It never had a layer for who you are and what you bring when two things meet: your identity, your assets, your credentials, your preferences, and the permissions you set between things that meet. Universal Manifest is that layer.

Universal Manifest is an open specification for the silent handshake between any two entities — people, devices, agents, organizations. A portable, signed envelope of context that any compatible system can read, verify, and act on. Every exchange leaves both sides with a structured receipt.

## Status

Approved OMA3 standards track. Initial implementations available. Specification and use case documentation at [universalmanifest.net](https://universalmanifest.net).
